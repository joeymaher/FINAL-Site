OUR LOCATIONS

NEILBOLT STREET

One for the all Stephen King fans and classic horror lovers. Our Neibolt Street tour gives fans a chance to creep through the terrifying house where it's said that all the strange happenings and dissapearances in Derry began.

Let Pennywise welcome you into his home and lure you into a truly immersive experience packed with gruesome thrills. Don't get lost along the way...

HOTEL TRANSYLVANIA

Live like the demi-dead and enjoy a night to remember at Dracula’s favourite getaway. Not your average weekend away, Hotel Transylvania offers a historic tour and peak into the secrets of ‘the unholy land’.

Come out at night for vampire themed masquerade party and after an evening of bloody gore and gothic delight check in and stay the night- We guarantee you’ll sleep like the dead!

THE OVERLOOK HOTEL

Submerge yourself in the Shining experience as we invite you to delve into the troublesome history of The Overlook Hotel.

Ghostly hallways, mischievous bar staff and don’t forget to to introduce yourself to some of the long-stay residents. And if it all gets too much, take a stroll in around the eerily tranquil gardens and our elaborate maze, you might just lose yourself in it.

AMITYVILLE

Whatever you do, get in and out of the Amityville House within the time alotted to you. Some have been known to disappear within those walls and never be seen again...

Probably the most gruesome of our tours, Amityville has the most real life horrors associated with it. Learn of the horror that befell the Lutz family and how it's gruesome events could never be explained. If the house calls you... run.
